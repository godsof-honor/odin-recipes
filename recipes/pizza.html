Pizza Recipe

A pepperoni pizza is a classic dinner idea that is fun for the family!

Using this recipe you can have food on the table in under 1 hour.

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1/4 teaspoon white sugar
  • 3/4 cup lukewarm water
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps:

  1. Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water that's 100 degrees F (44 degrees C) in a small bowl. Let stand until yeast softens and begins to form a creamy foam, about 5 to 8 minutes.
  2. Combine 1 3/4 cups flour and salt in a large bowl. Pour in yeast mixture; mix well until dough comes together.
  3. Transfer dough to a floured surface and knead until smooth, about 2 minutes. Add remaining 1/4 cup flour if dough is too sticky.
  4. Roll dogh into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to a greased pizza pan.

Baking:

Top pizza crust as desired and bake at 500 degrees F (260 degrees C) until golden, 8 to 10 minutes.
